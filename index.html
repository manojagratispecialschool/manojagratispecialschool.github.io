About Us

This NGO is dedicated to helping children who are struggling with mental illness, as well as their caregivers who are often overwhelmed and unsure of how to best support their child's needs. They provide a wide variety of resources and support to educate parents, teachers, and healthcare providers about different mental illnesses and developmental disabilities such as IDD, CP, HI, Down syndrome,mental retardation , hearing illness, visual impairment, ASD, and ADHD, along with their associated symptoms and challenges.

Vision


" Our aim is to promote mental health awareness and prevent mental health issues within our nation, while upholding important values that foster a healthy society. "

We are a school for mental health and wellness


Our school provides a safe and nurturing environment for students to learn, grow, and heal. With a focus on mental health and wellness, we offer a range of specialized programs and services to empower our students to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.


Mission


We provide assistance for individuals to empower themselves through both group and individual counseling sessions. Additionally, we conduct workshops and training programs to raise awareness about mental health.
